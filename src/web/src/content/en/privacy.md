# Privacy Policy for Qibla Now

**Effective date:** 2026-01-01  
**Website:** https://qibla-now.com  
**Application:** Qibla Now (Android)

## Overview

Qibla Now is designed as a focused religious utility that respects user privacy. This application does not require accounts, does not collect personal profiles, and does not operate a backend service for core functionality.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the app may access, how it is used, and what external services may be involved.

## 1. Data Collection

Qibla Now **does not collect or store personal data on its own servers**.

The application operates primarily **offline** and performs calculations directly on the device.

The following types of data are **not collected**:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Location history
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Behavioral tracking data

No user account or registration is required to use the application.

## 2. Location Information

The application may request access to the device's **precise location** in order to:

- Calculate prayer times
- Determine the direction of the Qibla

Location data is used **only locally on the device** for these calculations and is **not transmitted to the developer or stored remotely**.

Users may alternatively choose to manually set a location instead of granting location access.

## 3. Notifications

If enabled by the user, the application schedules **local notifications** on the device to alert for prayer times.

These notifications are generated and managed entirely on the device. No remote notification service is used by the application.

## 4. Internet Access

Most features of Qibla Now work without an internet connection.

Internet access may be used for optional features such as:

- Loading map tiles when viewing the map screen
- Displaying advertisements if ads are enabled in the application

These external services may process network requests according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Advertising

Qibla Now may display **non-personalized advertisements**.

Ads are limited to banner formats and are configured to avoid behavioral profiling.

Advertising services may collect limited technical information required to display ads, such as:

- Device type
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- Advertising identifiers provided by the operating system

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Users can reset or limit advertising identifiers in their device settings.

## 6. Data Storage

All user preferences and configuration settings are stored **locally on the device**.

This includes:

- Selected prayer calculation method
- Madhab preference
- Location settings
- Notification configuration
- Display preferences

This information is not transmitted to the developer.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Qibla Now does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Because the application does not collect personal data or require accounts, it is suitable for general audiences.

## 8. Third-Party Services

The application may interact with third-party services such as:

- Map providers
- Advertising networks

These services may process network requests independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.

## 9. Security

Since Qibla Now does not operate a backend service and stores data locally on the device, the risk of remote data exposure is minimized.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their device.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the application.

Updates will be published on the official website: https://qibla-now.com/privacy
