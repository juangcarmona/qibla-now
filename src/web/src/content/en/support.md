## Support

For support questions, feature requests, or bug reports, you can reach out through the following channels.

### In-app feedback

Use the feedback option in the app: **Settings → Feedback**.

### App store

Leave a review or send a message through the app store listing.

### Privacy inquiries

For privacy-related questions, see the [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy).

---

Qibla Now is maintained by a single developer. Response times may vary.
